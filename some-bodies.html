Website Design, 2024

The digital counterpart to "soup", this website extends the two-part series into an interactive space.

Beyond retelling the zine’s stories, it invites users to create their own “autopsy reports,” which get sent to me and may inspire future drinks and cases. The site also includes a mood-based language system, letting visitors shift the tone of the experience—another playful way of practicing death online.
