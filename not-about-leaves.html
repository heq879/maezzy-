"not everything about leaf and tree" is a collection of literary fragments and a printed gallery that explores how leaves capture the essence of nature, history, and the city itself.

The book contains leaves gathered from urban spaces, where nature exists alongside the bustle of city life but remains unnoticed by many. Each leaf, like a page in a book, holds a slice of the tree's lifeâ€”a a year, a season, a moment in the city's rhythm.

These fallen leaves, unabsorbed by the soil, witness the passage of time and tell their own stories. The book is a memento of time passing, and the lives that intertwined with it.
