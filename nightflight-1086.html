Night-Flight 1086

12"x24"
Book Installation, 2026

"night-Flight 1086 draws on my extensive journal archive to chronicle memories of dreams and life experiences from my childhood.

Alongside chapters collecting family snapshots and texts, I interspersed fabric pages representing the pillowcases of my youth. These textile dividers function as soft boundaries of the unconscious — portals through which dreams take form.

In this way, the work investigates how memory exists simultaneously across material and subconscious dimensions, transforming “recording” into a triggerable experience, a sensory encounter that unfolds involuntarily.
