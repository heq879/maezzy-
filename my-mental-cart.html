Website Design, 2024

"check out my mental cart" is a website designed around the theme of time.

Inspired by the concept of "time is money," and connecting it with everyday purchase receipts, "check out my mental cart" explores the importance of time management and the reality of facing multiple tasks and pressures in modern life.

The website reflects my mental state over the course of a week and the energy required for various activities. Each activity and task is assigned a "cost," providing a tangible visualization of time consumption, allowing users to intuitively grasp the value of time and the importance of managing it.
